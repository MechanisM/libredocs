Libre Docs alpha

Liberate your ideas

Write online, from anywhere, with your friends. Easily keep track of your documents instead of being tracked and trapped by the web's monopolies. We neither store your data nor track you. Libre Docs is only the application – your data is stored where you want it to be, using the open remoteStorage protocol.

 

Checking …

1. sign in with BrowserID

We use BrowserID, a great new technology to ease sign in, driven by our friends at Mozilla. Find out more.

2. get remoteStorage

You'll get an account at Iris Couch to use as your »home folder for the web«. We work on making other storage providers compatible.

3. write freely!

Apps are existing open web technologies. Starting out with Etherpad, soon adding WebODF, SVG-edit and more.

Libre Docs is a free software app by Unhosted for a more open web.
